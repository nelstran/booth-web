## Creating a session

<div markdown="1" class="step">
<p markdown="1">1.To start, click the blue '+' button at the bottom of your screen to navigate to the creation page.</p>
<img src="{{site.url}}{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/guide/home-page-session.png" alt="Home page with arrow pointing at create session" height=500>
</div>

<div markdown="1" class="step">
<p markdown="1">2.From there, fill in the appropriate information</p>
<div markdown="1" class="sessionDesc">
<img src="{{site.url}}{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/guide/create-session.png" alt="Session creation page" height=500>
<div markdown="1" class="fields">
Confused what each field means?
- **Title:** *Name your session, what main idea do you want your session to convey?*
- **Description:** *Go more in depth! What are you studying about? What will you be going over?*
- **Time:** *Set a time when your study session is going to start and when it is going to end*
- **Location Description:** *Where are you? What building are you in? Describe your surroundings! This will help others find you easier. You also have the ability to send a picture of your surroundings*
- **Seats Available:** *Reserved a table or room? Make sure to set a max limit of users in your session to avoid overcrowding.*
- **Class Abbreviation** *What class is this session for? This will help users look for sessions that they need*
- **Public/Private** *For convenience, users are able to create a session only their friends can see*
- **Location** *Along with the location description, we give users the option to share their current location which allows their session to be viewed on the map
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div markdown="1" class="step">
<p markdown="1"> 3.Click 'Create Session', your session will now be available for other users to see and join</p>
<img src="{{site.url}}{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/guide/home-page-made-session.png" alt="New session shown on the main page" height=500>
</div>
<br>

<div markdown="1" class="step">

<img src="{{site.url}}{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/guide/delete-session.png" alt="Dialog asking users to " height=500>
</div>
<div markdown="1" class="step">

## Joining/Leaving a session

### Joining

<div markdown="1" class="step">
<p markdown="1"> 1.Find a session you like? Click on their tile to see more details.</p>
<img src="{{site.url}}{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/guide/join-session.png" alt="Session details page" height=500>
</div>
<div markdown="1" class="step">
<div markdown="1"> 2.Click 'Join' and start heading towards their location!

### Leaving

<div markdown="1" class="step">
<div markdown="1">-  When you are done, you can leave the session the same way you joined. This will remove you from the group and you will no longer be able to message in the chat room
